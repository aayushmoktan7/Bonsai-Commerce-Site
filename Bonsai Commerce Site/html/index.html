What Makes A Plant A Bonsai?


What a Bonsai Tree exactly? Bonsai Plants are most commonly kept under four feet (a meter) but they are not genetically dwarfed plants. It is done by using artificial methods. Various types of techinques such as purning and wiring branches, carefully restricting fertilizers but not completely abondending or pinching buds are used to limit the growth of these plants.

The Symbolism Of Bonsai Plant

Bonsai Plant requires a lot of attention after its plantation, for it to survive and grow properly. Bonsai Plant has a lot of meanings on itself when you are gifting it to someone. It is generally symbolizes harmony, peace, balance in life and all of these things are good in nature, so Bonsai Plant makes great gifts.
